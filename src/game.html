How to play

  • 1. The players take turns to roll the dice.
  • 2. Move ahead the number of dots on the dice.
  • 3. If you land on a trap, you must retreat.
  • 4. When you roll a six, you get a bonus turn.
  • 5. The first one to reach the crown wins.
